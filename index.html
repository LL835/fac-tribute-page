Florence + the Machine

Florence + the Machine are an English indie rock band based in London. Known for their genre-bending sound and front woman Florence Welch's powerful vocals, the band has been met with both critical and popular acclaim.

Florence + the Machine have recorded five studio albums to date:

  • Lungs (2009)
  • Ceremonials (2011)
  • How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful (2015)
  • High as Hope (2018)
  • Dance Fever (2022)

Their latest album Dance Fever released in May 2022 and the Dance Fever Tour is set to begin later in the year. Check out Florence + the Machine's official website for information about tour dates.
